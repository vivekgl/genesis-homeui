Genesis Real Estate is one of the leading professional real estate companies of India,
and the largest owners of Real Estate in
Bangalore.

Genesis Real Estate is among the most respected full-spectrum developers of India, and one of the largest land
bank owners of the Industry with over 3000 acres of land and a Grade-A pipeline of over 20 Mn. sq.ft. under
development. With more than 40 years of industry leadership, Genesis Real Estate has come to be recognized
in the industry, as one of the oldest, most trusted and reliable brands for partners, investors, and homeowners
alike. Over the years, the growth of Genesis Real Estate has come to mirror the growth of Bengaluru as one of
Asia's fastest-growing global metro hubs.
